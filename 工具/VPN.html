VPN

使用秋水逸冰的脚本搭建VPN
  1. root用户登录
  2. wget --no-check-certificate https://raw.githubusercontent.com/teddysun/shadowsocks_install/master/shadowsocksR.sh
  3. chmod +x shadowsocksR.sh
  4. ./shadowsocksR.sh 2>&1 | tee shadowsocksR.log
    常用命令
  5. 启动：/etc/init.d/shadowsocks start
  6. 停止：/etc/init.d/shadowsocks stop
  7. 重启：/etc/init.d/shadowsocks restart
  8. 状态：/etc/init.d/shadowsocks status
  9. 配置文件路径：/etc/shadowsocks.json
  10. 日志文件路径：/var/log/shadowsocks.log
  11. 代码安装目录：/usr/local/shadowsocks
